About Us

Open -Source Software Center at SLU was established to help SLU researchers with their custom software needs. Our  Center is funded by a grant from Alfred P. Sloan foundation and employs Computer Science graduate students to prototype, design, and deliver open-source software to help SLU researchers and their collaborators worldwide. All software produced by this center will be open-source - it will be publicly available via github.com .
